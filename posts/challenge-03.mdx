A Link Preview is a component that enhances links to an external website by showing a thumbnail of the website when the user hovers over the link. Its purpose is to give the user some context of their destination before they leave your website.

The component is inspired by <LinkPreview url="https://uiw.tf/link-preview">uiw.tf</LinkPreview> and uses <LinkPreview url="https://microlink.io/">Microlink</LinkPreview> to capture the screenshot of the website and <LinkPreview url="https://radix-ui.com/primitives/docs/components/hover-card">Radix UI</LinkPreview> for the tooltip UI.
